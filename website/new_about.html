Intro


My name is Devan and I am currrently a Senior My current passion in the Software world is general purpose programming languages like Java and C/C++. I hope to get a fundamental mastery of these languages in the near future. I have studied C for 1 year and Java for 2 years in college. I like how easy Java is to manage memory but I appreciate the performance boost from being able to manual do Garbage Collection with C/C++.

I choose web tech this summer because I wanted to supplement my college education with web tech because I have not learned much web tech at school. I have primarily been focused with HTML5 and CSS3 with a bit of JavaScript.


The Past 7 years


After I graduated HighSchool in 2010 I enrolled in a local community college at Catawba Valley Community College. From there I went on to work full-time and go to school full-time with the goal of transfering to a university. My GPA from the first 2 1/2 years was a 2.1 GPA. I then went on to average a 3.8 GPA for the final 3 years as I started to take my education more seriously and fell in love with Science. I took Biology, Chemistry, Cacl 1,2,3 and Calc-based physics. My strong performance in the STEM classes my last three years of community college got me accepted to NCSU's Computer Science department in the fall of 2015.

These past two years I have taken a much more rigourous corriculum from Intro to Java to Operating Systems in Java and C. I have taken 4 Java programming classes, 2 C / C++, and 1 Assembly Language. This past year I tried to get an internship but ultimately failed but I finished a strong semester in the spring of 2017 with a 3.8 GPA average. My cumalitive GPA is currently around 3.4 at NCSU. My lowest grade is a B-.
